# 📚 FinBot Knowledge Base — PDF Downloads

Place your financial PDF documents in this folder.
These PDFs will be loaded, chunked, and stored in ChromaDB by `rag/ingest.py`.

---

## 📥 Recommended PDFs to Download (All Free & Official)

### 1. RBI — Reserve Bank of India
- **RBI Annual Report**
- URL: https://www.rbi.org.in/Scripts/AnnualReportPublications.aspx
- Save as: `rbi_annual_report.pdf`

### 2. SEBI — Investor Education
- **SEBI Investor Awareness Guide**
- URL: https://investor.sebi.gov.in
- Save as: `sebi_investor_guide.pdf`

### 3. Income Tax Department
- **ITR Filing Guide / Tax Slabs**
- URL: https://www.incometax.gov.in/iec/foportal/help/individual
- Save as: `income_tax_guide.pdf`

### 4. AMFI — Mutual Fund Guide
- **AMFI Investor Education PDF**
- URL: https://www.amfiindia.com/investor-corner/investor-education
- Save as: `amfi_mutual_fund_guide.pdf`

### 5. IRDAI — Insurance Guide
- **IRDAI Consumer Education Booklet**
- URL: https://irdai.gov.in/consumer-education
- Save as: `irdai_insurance_guide.pdf`

---

## ✅ Minimum Required
At least **3 PDFs** are needed before running `rag/ingest.py`.

## ⚠️ Notes
- Scanned/image PDFs will NOT be parsed (text-based PDFs only)
- Each PDF should be under 50MB
- Rename files to simple names without spaces
